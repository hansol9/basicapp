Introducing the Cinematic World of Bong Joon-ho, a World-renowned Director

Director Bong Joon-ho captivates the world with his unique direction that transcends genre boundaries and his sharp social critiques through his films.

Parasite

2019

Winner of Best Picture, Best Director, Best Original Screenplay, and Best International Feature Film at the 92nd Academy Awards

Snowpiercer

2013

Class struggle unfolds aboard a moving train during an ice age caused by climate change.

The Host

2006

The story of a family battling a monster that appears in the Han River.

Mother

2009

A mystery thriller exploring powerful maternal love.

Key Awards and Honors

  • 4-time Oscar Winner (Parasite)
  • Palme d'Or at the Cannes Film Festival (Parasite)
  • Jury Prize at the Berlin Film Festival (Okja)
